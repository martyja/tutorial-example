About

Hello! My name is Jenny Marty. I am a Media Arts and Design major at James Madison University. My concentration is Interactive Design, and I have minors in Art and Entrepreneurship. I am a designer, artist, creator, and curator. I have a passion for making things beautiful and functional. Below you will find some of my work in interactive design, graphic design, and photography.

Experience

As part of my course work in Media Arts and Design, I have developed skills of writing and understanding HTML and CSS coding, utilizing mobile first design, the CSS grid, and HTML5 semantic elements. I have also learned how to use the Adobe Creative Cloud to make practical designs using Gestalt principles. Additionally, I have gained experience in photography, specifically black and white film photography. Below you will find more information and examples of each of these skills.
